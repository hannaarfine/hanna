My Contribution

At Amazon, I had the opportunity to work with the Advertising Experience and Technology Design Technology team to help design, proptotype, and develop new advertising experiences. My intern project was to research, design, prototype, and build an experience that allows customers to shop multiple items on the Fire Tablet Wakescreen.

The Goal

Amazon's Fire Tablet Wakescreen ad format is a great space for advertisers to feature a product, given its prime real estate and large canvas for visually appealing and engaging displays. However, its current version is limited, as it is best suited to feature only a single item.

My intern project goal was to create a shoppable lifestyle format ad that allows customers to shop complete looks and add to cart mulitple items from the wakescreen itself.

UX Research

Researched and created persona to guide design & development

Created 5 different paper prototypes for low fidelity testing

I used these 5 sets of paper prototypes to conduct a round of internal user testing. These results informed my high fidelity prototype design decisions.

4 high fidelity Sketch & InVision prototypes

I conducted another round of internal user testing, this time with the high fidelity prototypes.

Development

To develop the product, I committed a few days to learning React & Redux so that I could make an accessible, reusable codebase that could be easily adapted after I went back to school.

The codebase relies upon the Amazon API to get realtime stock, price, and options of the featured products on the ad. This allows the customer to have reliale information about the product and complete their add to cart action on the wakescreen.

All blue dots indicate a component supplied in real time by the Amazon API.

Final Product

The final product allows advertisers to feature multiple ASINS for a complete look. They can feature any number of products, and the codebase can be easily updated for future campaigns.

As of now, Shop the Look has already been sold to 3 major companies, with campaigns set to launch in the coming months.

I'm extremely grateful to have had the opportunity to work with such a supportive & creative team of people, and I can't wait to return to Amazon as a full time Design Technologist in Fall 2019!
