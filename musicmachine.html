Music Machine

Worked with team of 3 to build a marble-dropping music machine that plays back songs based on analog user input.

TypeSchool project | Cornell University
ProcessConcepting, Wireframing, Design, Prototyping
ToolsArduino, 3D Printing, Laser Cutting

Spring 2018

Over the course of a semester, Kyler Ruvane, Eric Gendreau and I were tasked with building a complex rapid prototyping project using Arduino, 3D printing, and laser cutting. In response, we made a music machine that drops marbles onto a xylophone. It reads hand-drawn input from a grid of notes, then plays the corresponding tune.
