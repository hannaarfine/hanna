TypeHackathon
RoleUI/UX Designer

Winter 2019

I spent January of 2019 in Tel Aviv as a TavTech design fellow, a fellowship that connects students to Israel's economy by offering access to Israel's top entrepreneurs through an engaging educational program, giving them tangible, high-demand technical skills.

After 4 weeks of courses in design and prototyping, we collaborated for a 3-day Google Startups Hackathon in which we were tasked with solving a global social problem.

Translating care for social causes into action



When we polled our peers, 89% said they cared about social causes, yet only 43% have ever donated to a cause.

We aimed to create a platform that features:

  • Aggregation of nonprofits
  • Ease of donating
  • Financial transparancy
  • Social media engagement
  • Personal curation


Solution: a subscrtiption-based platform that allows for streamlined, transparent donations to causes you care about.
